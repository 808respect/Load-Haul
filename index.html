    ABOUT US

    THE STORY OF WHO WE ARE

    Founded in 2008 and focused on providing cross-border transport across Zimbabwe, Zambia, and South Africa. We are a BEE Level 6 certified firm, able to offer our clients various options with regards to collection and delivery destinations. We have a sister company in Zimbabwe operating three triaxles

    We have built up a wide customer base with our well-trained, well-presented and well- mannered drivers. We operate a fully equipped workshop with highly trained staff in Zimbabwe en-route for both companies with a 24 hours call out facility.

    We are a small business which manages to do our best to provide a personal and professional service.

    GARIAN AGRI SERVICES PVT LTD T/A PRIMTRACK TRADING

    OUR SISTER COMPANY OPERATING IN ZIMBABWE

    Founded in 1989. We have been providing local and cross-border transportation of farm produce and various commodities. We mainly are within Zimbabwe and do transport when the harvest season is on nationwide. We're able to or our clients various options with regards to collection and delivery destinations.

    We are also John Deere Dealers specializing in sales, repairs and maintenance of farming equipment. At Primtrack Trading, you get the value of choice for your replacement and maintenance parts for all makes and ages of machines - at any budget. Search parts for your tractors, lawn mowers, agricultural equipment, and more to get the right part for the right price.
